Willow Wood was added with the [Swamp](notes/makeover/swamp) makeover.

It is found on [Willow](notes/generation/willow) trees throughout the biome.

Like all other wood types it comes with:  
- Log
- Wood
- Stripped Log
- Stripped Wood
- Planks
- Slab
- Stairs
- Fence
- Fence Gate
- Pressure Plate
- Button
- Trapdoor
- Door
- Sign
- Boat
- Boat with Chest
- Sapling
- Leaves
